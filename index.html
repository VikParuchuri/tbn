TURN YOUR SELF-TRACKING DATA INTO ACTIONABLE INSIGHT.

Glimpse, a new open source tool, is going to change the way you use your tracking data. Help us make Glimpse the best it can be.

The quantified self tool you have been waiting for

For trackers

Get all of your data in one place The data we track is scattered across dozens of different tools right now. Glimpse lets you unify your data in one secure place.

Set goals and run experiments Want to figure out if using facebook less will improve your mood? Glimpse can leverage cross-platform data to make it happen.

Create comprehensive metrics Ever wanted to combine your activity on different social networks to create a social score? Or figure out how much you are browsing the internet across all of your devices every day? Glimpse lets you do it.

Customize and tweak Glimpse is open source, which means that you can host it yourself and customize it however you want.

For developers

Stop worrying about the backend Glimpse allows you to write plugins for your app, which expose API endpoints. Use these endpoints to store and retrieve data without the hassle of building your own system.

Let users track correlations Do your users want to compare data from your app with data from another one? Instead of writing your own integration, leverage our existing plugins to let them do it with no additional effort.

Monetize your plugin Glimpse supports one-time and recurring billing, so you can sell your plugin if you want.

Use your own brand Glimpse fully supports branding, so if you decide to host it yourself, you can customize it to stay consistent with the look and feel of your app.
